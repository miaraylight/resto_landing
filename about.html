Our Story

Founded in 1998 by the Ramirez family, La Catrina is built on traditional recipes passed down for generations. What started as a small kitchen in a Chicago neighborhood has grown into a beloved spot for locals and visitors alike.

Our Mission

To bring the rich, vibrant flavors of Mexico to our community using fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Every dish tells a story, and we are proud to serve food that’s made with love, care, and authentic spices.

Meet the Family

Maria Ramirez, our head chef, learned her techniques from her grandmother in Oaxaca. Her son, Diego, manages the restaurant and ensures every guest feels like part of the family.
